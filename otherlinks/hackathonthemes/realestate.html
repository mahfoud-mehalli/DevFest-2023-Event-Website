Real Estate

Welcome to the Real Estate Sector of our DevFest Hackathon! The real estate industry is on the brink of a technological revolution, and participants in this sector will explore how innovation can disrupt and transform traditional practices.

Potential for Technological Disruption: From smart property management to blockchain-based transactions, the potential for technological disruption in real estate is immense. Participants will have the opportunity to tackle challenges that reimagine how we buy, sell, and manage properties in the digital age.

What to Expect: Participants can expect to work on problematics that involve smart property matching, automated valuation models, and blockchain solutions for transparent and secure transactions. This sector encourages participants to envision a future where technology enhances efficiency and accessibility in real estate.
